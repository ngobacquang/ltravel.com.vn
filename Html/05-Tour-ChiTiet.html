Itinerary

DAY 1 - HCMC - MADAGUI - DA LAT (Breakfast, lunch, dinner)

In the morning: Tour and Vietnamese Tour Guide pick you up at the meeting point of departure to travel to fall in Dalat. Have breakfast at Nga Dau Dau cross. Continue to depart to TP. Da Lat.
Stop to visit Madagui tourist area, with a collection of bamboos of nearly 50 different types, discover the mysterious system of natural caves, nature reserve with many wild animals living naturally.


Freedom to explore thrilling game systems such as kayaking, zipline, mountain climbing, aerial walking, skiing, horseback riding, paintball shooting, etc. (expenses excluded)


Lunch: Have lunch at the Muong Xanh restaurant. Continue to visit Truc Lam Zen Monastery, take the cable car through Rôbin hill (expenses excluded), admire the pine forest, Tuyen Lam lake, Phuong Hoang mountain from above.


Evening: Have dinner, check in and rest. You are self-sufficient walking around the city of Da Lat at night, admire the scenery of Xuan Huong Lake, enjoy the taste of coffee in the mountain town (self-sufficient). Overnight hotel in Da Lat.
